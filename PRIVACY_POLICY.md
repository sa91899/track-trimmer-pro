# Privacy Policy for Track Trimmer Pro

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

Track Trimmer Pro ("we," "our," or "the application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our desktop application.

## Data Collection and Processing

**Track Trimmer Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.**

All audio processing performed by Track Trimmer Pro occurs entirely on your local machine. The application:

- Processes audio files locally using FFmpeg
- Does not require an internet connection for core functionality
- Does not send any data to external servers
- Does not track user activity or usage patterns
- Does not collect personal information, audio file content, or metadata
- Does not use analytics or telemetry services

## Local File Processing

When you use Track Trimmer Pro to process audio files:

- All processing is performed on your device
- Audio files remain on your local storage
- No audio content is transmitted over the internet
- No processing data is stored in cloud services

## Third-Party Services

Track Trimmer Pro uses the following open-source libraries:

- **FFmpeg**: Used for local audio processing. FFmpeg operates entirely on your device and does not transmit data externally.
- **Electron**: The application framework. Electron does not collect user data by default.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the support email provided in the application's About section.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. All your data remains under your complete control on your local device.
