Track Trimmer Pro Support Getting Started How to use Track Trimmer Pro: Add Audio: Click the "Single File" button to select one file or "Folder" for multiple audio tracks from your Mac. Click "Browse" to choose your input file/folder. Click "Browse to choose your output destination. ** Note ** You should always choose a different destination folder from your input folder as the program will try to save the file in the same place which may overwrite the original file! Otherwise, you can add a prefix or suffix to all your files at the bottom of tghe screen. Process Settings Set Markers: Use the up/down arrows or type in a manual time entry to define your Start and End points. You can increment time by 10ths of a second. Set the fade in/out times if desired Audio Enhancements You can optionally enable Normalization to balance volume. Choose Format Select your desired output format (MP3, WAV, FLAC, etc.) and bitrate. You can also chose your desired Audio Quality Settings Add any prefix or suffix to your filename. For example, if you are creating 30 second clips of your audio files, you could put _30second as your suffix, and all your files will be saved with that suffix. You can use these fields to differentiate your processed files from their originals. Process: Click the "Process" button. Your trimmed files will be saved to your chosen destination folder. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) Q: Does Track Trimmer Pro support batch editing? A: Yes! You can select multiple files at once. The settings you choose (trims, fades, and format) will be applied to every file in your queue simultaneously. Q: Where are my files processed? A: Everything happens 100% locally on your Mac. We never upload your audio to the cloud, ensuring your privacy and much faster processing speeds. Q: What formats can I export to? A: You can export to MP3, WAV, M4A, FLAC, OGG, and AAC. Q: Why isn't my file loading? A: Ensure the file is not corrupted and that it is a supported audio format. If you continue to have issues, please contact us with the file type you are using. Q: How do you process a new project? A: To adjust settings for a new project, simply enter new values into the time fields. These will automatically apply to your next processing task. If you prefer, you can close & restart the app for each project. Contact & Feedback We are constantly working to improve Track Trimmer Pro.
