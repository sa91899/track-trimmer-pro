# Track Trimmer Pro Support

## Getting Started
**How to use Track Trimmer Pro:**

* **Add Audio:** Click the **"Single File"** button to select one file or **"Folder"** for multiple audio tracks from your Mac.
* **Select Input:** Click **"Browse"** to choose your input file/folder.
* **Select Output:** Click **"Browse"** to choose your output destination. 

> **Note:** You should always choose a different destination folder from your input folder. If you save to the same location, the program may overwrite the original file. Alternatively, use the prefix/suffix fields to differentiate your files.

---

## Process Settings

* **Set In/Out Points:** Use the up/down arrows or type in a manual time entry to define your **Start** and **End** points. You can increment time by 10ths of a second.
* **Fades:** Set the fade in/out times if desired.
* **Audio Enhancements:** You can optionally enable **Normalization** to balance volume.
* **Choose Format:** Select your desired output format (MP3, WAV, FLAC, etc.), bitrate, and Audio Quality Settings.
* **Naming:** Add any **prefix or suffix** to your filename. For example, if you are creating 30-second clips, you could use `_30second` as your suffix.
* **Process:** Click the **"Process"** button. Your trimmed files will be saved to your chosen destination folder.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Does Track Trimmer Pro support batch editing?**
**A:** Yes! You can select multiple files at once. The settings you choose (trims, fades, and format) will be applied to every file in your queue simultaneously.

**Q: Where are my files processed?**
**A:** Everything happens 100% locally on your Mac. We never upload your audio to the cloud, ensuring your privacy and much faster processing speeds.

**Q: What formats can I export to?**
**A:** You can export to MP3, WAV, M4A, FLAC, OGG, and AAC.

**Q: Why isn't my file loading?**
**A:** Ensure the file is not corrupted and that it is a supported audio format. If you continue to have issues, please contact us with the file type you are using.

**Q: How do you process a new project?**
**A:** To adjust settings for a new project, simply enter new values into the time fields. These will automatically apply to your next processing task. If you prefer, you can close and restart the app for each project.
